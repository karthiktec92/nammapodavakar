Our Story

Fashion With Purpose

Founded in 2018, Namma Ooru Podavakar was born from a simple belief — that everyone deserves to wear clothes that make them feel confident, comfortable, and truly themselves.

Who We Are

Built on Passion, Driven by Style

Namma Ooru Podavakar started as a small boutique in the streets of Chennai, with just a few carefully selected pieces. Today, we've grown into a trusted local fashion destination — but our core values remain unchanged.

We believe fashion should be inclusive, sustainable, and accessible. Every piece in our collection is handpicked for quality, comfort, and style. We work directly with ethical manufacturers to ensure every garment is made responsibly.

From the first stitch to the final button, we obsess over the details so you can wear your story with confidence.
